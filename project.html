Core Engineering Advantages
  • Rapid Actuation & Response: High-thrust pneumatic (piston-driven) actuators achieve full stroke within 1–2 seconds, ideal for emergency isolation and high-speed process control.
  • Minimized Pressure Drop: 45° Y-pattern body design ensures streamlined flow path, high Cv value, and improved pumping efficiency.
  • Exceptional Durability: Angled stem design reduces fluid impact on critical components, increasing service life even in erosive or high-temperature applications.

  • Bubble-Tight Sealing: Soft-seat or Metal-to-Metal seating ensures zero leakage and strict safety compliance.

Our valves are selected considering critical operating parameters such as media type, pressure, temperature, and voltage compatibility, ensuring optimal performance in diverse industrial environments.

Multi-Louver & Damper Valves

Fabricated Multi-Louver and Butterfly Dampers are essential components in industrial ducting systems for regulating air, flue gas, and other low-pressure media. While both are designed for flow control, they differ in construction, control precision, and application suitability.

Product Variants
  • Fabricated Butterfly Damper Valve (Single Blade): Uses a single rotating disc mounted on a central shaft. Cost-effective solution for round ducts where full shut-off or coarse modulation is required.
  • Fabricated Multi-Louver Damper: Comprises multiple blades mounted on parallel shafts, ideal for high-volume airflow and precise modulation in large rectangular or round ducts.
